## Football Basketball Volleyball Badminton Team Training Information Management System

> [Click me download source code / Try it online](https://www.devquizdone.online/detail/6aba603c1b7a459e919b32841be37232/ghb20250919) 

### 1. Related Technologies
- Backend: `Java`, `JavaWeb` / `Springboot`.
- Frontend: `Vue`, `HTML` / `CSS` / `Javascript`, etc.
- Database: `MySQL`

### 2. Related Software (Any one of the listed software can be used to run)
- `IDEA`
- `Eclipse`
- `Visual Studio Code(VScode)`
- `Navicat`
- etc.

### 3. Feature Description
The system is divided into (roles): regular users, administrators.

**Coach Features:**
1. Login
2. Registration
3. Homepage
4. Personal Center (Change Password, Personal Information)
5. Join Team Management
6. Announcement Information Management
7. Team Information Management
8. Training Plan Management
9. Training Progress Management
10. Match Starting Lineup
11. Attendance Information Management

**Player Features:**
1. Login
2. Registration
3. Homepage
4. Personal Center (Change Password, Personal Information)
5. Join Team Management
6. Announcement Information Management
7. Training Progress Management
8. Match Starting Lineup
9. Team Information Management
10. Training Plan Management
11. Attendance Information Management


**Administrator Features:**
1. Login
2. Homepage
3. Personal Center (Change Password, Personal Information)
4. Administrator Management
5. Basic Data Management (Announcement Type Management, Team Type Management)
6. Coach Management
7. Join Team Management
8. Announcement Information Management
9. Team Information Management
10. Training Plan Management
11. Training Progress Management
12. Match Starting Lineup
13. Attendance Information Management
14. Player Management
